This is a UNDP Survey to assess the earning level of workers in various districts in Sierra Leone.
This is the ADMIN panel that visualizes the data collected from the form.
The data provided shows:
  1. Total number of Participants
  2. Districts that Participated in the Survey
  3. Amount sent to survey participants for participating
  4. Chart for Number Of Participants per Occupation
  5. Monthly Income of participants
  6. Average Monthly Earnings per Gender
  7. Average Monthly Earnings per District
  8. Average Monthly Earnings per Age Category
  9. Total Average Monthly Earnings
